Truemeds Editorial Policy


Our medical experts are committed to delivering you dependable, authoritative, and upright content.


Our editorial policy is founded on a commitment to deliver trustworthy health and medical information that meets the highest standards of accuracy and integrity. The information found on our website is created with deep research from professional medical experts in their respective fields. Our distinguished team of medical professionals comprises specialised doctors, pharmacists, and editors who boast unrivalled experience in both scientific research and medical writing. All content published on Truemeds is thoroughly reviewed by an experienced team of editors before it is released for publication.




Editorial Workflow


1. Planning & Research


Our editorial team works diligently to ensure that the content we provide is reliable and up-to-date. We conduct extensive research on each topic before creating the content. This includes validation from our clinical specialists and consulting reputable medical journals, studies, databases, and other resources.

2.Structure of the content


Carefully crafted structures are designed for each content category to guarantee uniformity and accuracy. Our editors strive to provide information in an
easily understandable form that free of jargon or technical language. We aim to make sure the reader gains a clear understanding of each topic.


3.Content Curation


We use a combination of automated keyword search technology and manual curation to ensure our content is relevant, up-to-date, and accurate. Our
team feed in news and updates from industry sources such as review articles, medical journals, meta-analysis, consensus statements, practice
guidelines, summary of product characteristics (SmPC), systematic reviews, and textbooks.



4.Content Creation


Our writers collaborate with healthcare editors and doctors to develop engaging yet factual pieces that meet our high-quality standards. We carefully review
each sentence for accuracy, grammar, readability, structure, and tone.
