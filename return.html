Cancelletion Policy



How and When Can I Cancel My Order?



Orders can be cancelled at any stage.


1. Pre-dispatch (Ready to ship)

The customer can cancel the order from the Android App/website:



Steps to cancel your order via the App/ Website-



Orders -> Select order -> Order details tab -> Cancel order or by calling our Customer Service Representative at +91-8069808861(Between 9am-9pm, all 7 days).



2. Post-Dispatch


Customer has to connect with the customer service team on +91-8069808861. The refund (only online) will only be processed post the order is marked as Returned to Origin. The timeline will vary depending upon
the status of the package. Usually this process will take about 5 to 7 business days. Refund will be initiated within 48hrs of Truemeds receiving the returned medicines.



Return Policy



How to Initiate a Return?



  • You can initiate a return request within 15 days of delivery by raising a ticket/request on the Android app/website from the "Help with my orders" section or by calling us on +91-8069808861.

  • Please check to see if the product(s) you wish to return are eligible for return (You can check if the product is returnable on the Product page of the item on either the app or the website).

  • Select the item you wish to return, specifying the quantity, reason for return
